Free Community Health Services (免费社区服务)

We provide essential healthcare services including blood pressure checks, basic diagnostics, and health consultations free of charge to underserved communities in Malaysia. These outreach events improve accessibility and early detection of health issues.

Mission Trips to Rural Areas

Our dedicated team regularly organizes mission trips to remote villages and underserved rural communities. We bring medical supplies, conduct health screenings, and provide essential healthcare education to families who have limited access to medical facilities.

Do what is needed! Care More!

Charity for Rural Families

Our organization channels funds and medical supplies to families in rural or hard-to-reach areas. These charity drives are coordinated with local NGOs to ensure aid reaches those most in need, especially during times of crisis or natural disasters.

Health Awareness Campaigns

We organize regular health awareness campaigns that address common illnesses, mental health challenges, and preventive care practices. These campaigns use accessible language and visuals to reach people of all literacy levels and languages.

Company Charity Fund

We believe in taking care of our own. Our internal charity fund supports CareMore employees and their families during times of unexpected medical hardship, ensuring our team remains strong and supported from within.

Digital Inclusion Drives: Virtual Reality Training for Healthcare

In collaboration with tech partners, we distribute basic digital tools and offer training workshops in rural clinics. With our organizations at the forefront of the modern technology, our cutting-edge initiative introduces virtual reality technology (VR) to healthcare training in rural communities. Local healthcare workers can practice complex procedures in a safe, simulated environment before performing them in real-life situations. This immersive learning approach bridges knowledge gaps and improves patient outcomes in areas with limited training resources!
